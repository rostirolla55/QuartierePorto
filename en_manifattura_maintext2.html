Current Status and New Function

Today, the area of the former Manifattura is part of a major architectural and urban redevelopment project known as **Manifattura delle Arti**.

  • Cineteca di Bologna: The historic building on Via Riva di Reno is the current home of the Cineteca di Bologna, an internationally renowned cultural center for the preservation and promotion of cinema.

  • Gardens: Near the former tobacco factory are the Giardini ex Manifattura Tabacchi, a green area open to the public (generally from 7:00 am to 7:00 pm), often frequented by families and featuring a children's play area.
